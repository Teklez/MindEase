— How it works

Three small steps. No pressure.

Most people open MindEase for the first time at 11pm, on a phone, after a hard day. The flow is built for that — short, kind, and asks only what helps.
01 / CHECK IN

Notice how you feel

One tap. Five faces. A short note if you want. We track the line, not the moment.

02 / TALK IT OUT

Talk, in your own words

Type or speak. MindEase listens, reflects back, and offers grounded techniques — never platitudes.

03 / SEE THE PATTERN

Watch the line settle

Streaks, gentle nudges, and trends help you see what's working — without turning wellness into a scoreboard.
